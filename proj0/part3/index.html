Part 3: The Dolly Zoom?

Dolly zoom = move the camera forward/backward while inversely zooming to keep the subject the same size, causing the background to expand/contract.

Clip

Steps

  1. Pick a subject centered in frame.
  2. Dolly (walk or slide) toward the subject while zooming out to maintain subject size (or the reverse).
  3. Keep horizon constant and camera level to emphasize background warping.

Notes

  • If you only have a fixed lens, fake it by taking multiple distances and simulate zoom with cropping (less ideal, but shows the idea).
  • Shoot at higher shutter speed to avoid motion blur while moving.
